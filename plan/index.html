Schedule

The schedule screen provides a view of the controls carried out and to be carried out.

The left part of the screen contains past and future checks. By clicking on a column, you are returned to the list of controls planned for the corresponding month.

The right side contains the checks scheduled for this month. It is possible to change the current month and select a control in the calendar.
